2026 Theme

What does "Smart Money Talks" mean for your students?

Set annually by the OECD and INFE, Global Money Week's 2026 theme calls on communities to open honest, stigma-free conversations about money. For UAE youth growing up in a digital, cashless world, these conversations have never mattered more.

From managing a first allowance to understanding digital payments and saving goals, the financial skills built this week will compound across a lifetime. Zoud and the Central Bank of the UAE are making it happen — at scale.

"Encouraging open dialogue about money helps young people build critical thinking, reduce financial anxiety, and avoid the costly mistakes that follow financial silence."
— Zoud × Central Bank of the UAE · Global Money Week 2026

A first-of-its-kind financial literacy platform for UAE students

Built in partnership with Zoud and the Central Bank of the UAE, our gamified platform turns financial education into a personalised adventure — meeting every student in their own world.

  • Personalised learning paths AI matches financial scenarios to student interests — football, gaming, music, and more
  • 3 interactive games Budget Blitz, Savings Quest, and Smart Spender — real-world skills through play
  • Live national leaderboard School, emirate, and national tiers — healthy competition amplifies motivation
  • Official certificates Branded Zoud × Central Bank UAE completion certificates, signed and downloadable
  • Badges, streaks & rewards Custom avatars, daily streaks, unlockable badges — students keep coming back
  • Arabic + English Fully bilingual — RTL Arabic and LTR English, switchable at any time
